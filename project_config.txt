project_name = desired name for your project

default settings configurations is local on these files:
*manage.py
*config/asgi.py
*config/wsgi.py

when copying apps:
*change the app name in apps.py into "apps.<name_of_app>"
*update the urlpatterns in config/urls.py
*update the INSTALLED_APPS in config/settings/base.py
*update your different setttings configurations in your config/settings
